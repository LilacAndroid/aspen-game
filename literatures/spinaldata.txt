A5P3101N SCHEMATIC DATA: Spinal Support Unit
// FOR CLASSIFIED EYES ONLY //
Due to the excessive pressure placed on the neck by the Central Processing Unit, the human body is unfit to contain and operate without modification without causing severe harm.
The Spinal Support Unit (SSU) is a remediation. By plating the spinal column with titanium and reinforced steel bars, proper locomotion can be achieved without harm to the patient.

Using what little power is generated by natural neural activity from the nervous system, we are able to power the CPU to a minimal capacity. This is by no means sustainable, requiring an external power source to remain online.
This allows this excess electrical signals to instead be harnessed as a temporary backup power source, in the rare cases that these units lose power during scouting operations.

The Spinal Support Unit is not made to be adjusted. It is meant to remain a permanent fixture, and any modification will require manual and persistent maintenence.
